Boiled Rice

Description

Very easy recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash rice with cold water
  2. Put 2 to 1, water to rice ratio
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Wait till it makes bubble's
  5. Lower the degrees at the lowest,put the lid on and wait 18 minutes

Done!
